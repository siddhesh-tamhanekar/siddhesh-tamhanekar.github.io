Contribute to UniqueValidator

It is not what we get. But who we become, what we contribute... that gives meaning to our lives. - Anthony Robbins

As an open source project, UniqueValidator thrives thanks to its community, and especially from volunteer developers who are willing to give some of their time to help resolve issues in the code.

Please consider contributing to the UniqueValidator in one or more of the ways outlined below. Contributions from users like you keep the project vibrant, alive, and on the path of progress.

Are you a User?
If you find an issue but do not know how to write code for it, please report it on the below contact email.

Are you a PHP Developer?
if you are php developer, you can help developement by fixing problems, adding more functionality.

Are you a front end developer?
Library is not currently supported client side validations but it going to support soon so if you are front end developer, you can help us in developement feel free to contact us.

Are you a good content writer?
We encourage you to add or correct UniqueValidator Documentation.

Do you have suggestions?
We always welcome the new innovative ideas and your valuable feedback, if you are interested then feel free to contact us.

Your contributions to UniqueValidator informed by your experience with using UniqueValidator are highly valued and appreciated. Let us know if you have any questions!
